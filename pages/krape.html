Krape Park

Krape Park Disc Golf Course, installed in 2003, is a 19 hole park-style course that flows through beautiful Krape Park on the westside of Freeport, IL. Despite its beauty, it can pose a challenge for all but the best of disc golfers!

Krape Park has become the home of two area tournament: Krape Park Doubles Presented by Kronk Disc Golf and the Krape Park Battle of the Birdies, a PDGA-sanctioned C-Tier event.

Krape Park Disc Golf course features several challenging wooded holes, holes with water hazards, tight fairways, and has several holes that allow players to put a good rip on a disc.

The course offers multiple trash cans along the route, passes many benches and picnic tables, and has easy bathroom accessibility for the 12 holes at the top of Flagstaff Hill. The bathrooms are most accessible from the parking lot as well as holes 7 - 9.

Location

Krape Park Disc Golf Course is located at Flagstaff Hill inside Krape Park.

You can learn more information about the course by visiting Udisc including course layout and scorecard.
