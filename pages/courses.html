Courses

Krape Park

Located at Flagstaff Hill, the Krape Park Disc Golf Course is a 19-hole course that winds its way through most of beautiful Krape Park. With tighter fairways and trees to contend with, accuracy will be rewarded. Learn more about Krape Park Disc Golf Course.

Highland

Located on the campus of Highland Community College, the Highland Disc Golf Course is built for longer throwers! With primarily wide-open fairways, it is a great course to unleash all your power! Learn more about Highland Disc Golf Course.
