Recent News

Freeport Area Putting League

The Cedarville Community Center will be hosting our Freeport Area Putting League on Thursdays at 5:30pm! The center is located at 430 W. Washington Street. Buyin will be $5/week and payouts will be decided each night based on turnout. A mystery box of discs will be available for the league points winner! The second and fourth weeks of February will be on Wednesday rather than Thursday (Feb. 8th & 22nd).

New Tee Pads at HCC

We have all wished for concrete teepads at the Highland Community College course, and now they are here! Special thanks goes out to the following for helping get these in the ground in just a week: Chris, Jp, Denny, Denny's friend (sorry, I'm bad at remembering names!), Big Pappa Kronk, Jon, Jason, Steve, and Dennis. Great work done!

GoFundMe for Course Upgrades

We are raising money via GoFundMe to make a number of updates to our course at Highland Community College. We are looking to add concrete teepads, new tee signs, new MVP Disc Sports baskets with padlocks, and new maps for easier navigation. Any business donation of $250 or more will be recognized with a sponsorship on one of our tee signs as well as recognition on our website. To make a donation, check out our GoFundMe page.
